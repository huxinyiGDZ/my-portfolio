Analysis report of customer shopping trend dataset

To help merchants better understand consumers' spending preferences, we categorized the shopping information of 3,900 consumers based on their attributes, including age, gender, item purchased, purchase frequency, location, and other tags.

First, for consumers in different regions, we compare "consumers favorite shopping seasons in each region" and "spending amount levels of consumers in different regions" to provide merchants with granular regional consumption trend forecasts. Second, for different genders, we compared "consumer category preferences" and "differences in shopping frequency". Finally, we also analyzed “consumers color preferences for different categories of products in different seasons” and “the impact of discounts on the amount spent”, hoping to help merchants predict the next stage of market consumption trends from multiple perspectives.

Tools used: SQLite, Python

Decoding Creative Content Trends Data Analysis Report

The goal of this project is to explore the most popular content categories and emerging trends by analyzing data from the top 1000 YouTubers, aiming to provide guidance for content creation and consumption for creators, MCNs, and brands. The specific questions involve identifying the most popular content categories and determining how to adjust video content and strategies to enhance account influence and user engagement. The motivation lies in addressing the challenge creators face in capturing user interests during content creation and meeting the demand for real-time awareness of trending content.
